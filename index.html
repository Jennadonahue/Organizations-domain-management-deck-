One Account, Multiple Brands: Four Problems That Don’t Go Away

Forcing multiple brands into one account creates four compounding problems — none of which go away with more features.

🔒 Consent gets crossed

One unsubscribe silences a customer across every brand — even ones they never opted out of.

📬 Reputation bleeds between brands

One brand’s spam complaints hurt deliverability for every other brand in the account.

📊 Metrics become meaningless

Revenue and engagement blend together — you can’t tell which brand is winning or losing.

⚙️ Config can’t scale per brand

Every brand shares the same domains, phone numbers, and settings — there’s no way to configure them independently.
